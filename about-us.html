About Us

We are a fast-growing agricultural technology company and financial institution, passionate about ensuring food and financial security. We combine savings and investment services with crop production and distribution; to create a more just and equitable world.

Our Mission is to:
• To making the word a healthier, greener and more connect place, through fresh food, smart saving and shared commitment to making a difference.
• To Build the largest community of farmers across Africa and ensure financial stability of her people. .

• OUR VISION

To create a sustainable world where everyone has access to healthy food, financial security, basic needs and brighter future. With our available resources, Africa has the capacity to help herself eat well, live well and save well for a better future.

OUR PRODUCTS AND SERVICES


★ Investment
Invest in sustainable future!. Earn up to 25% interest on the investment for 12 month duration. Join others to enjoy an interest rate 6 times higher than the bank offers.
i. Savings plan registration commences in October and closes January 5th every year. Savings deposit commences January (both monthly and weekly savings).
ii. Minimum months of savings is 11 months to earn 10% interest in your total savings.


★ Savings
We offer you 10% interest on your total savings in 11 months. Greenland savings plan help you plan ahead for your special celebration and goals, Create a budget and save towards buying a new appliance or raise your business capital with ease.
i. Investment deposit commences in October and closes December 30th every year.
ii. Minimum investment amount is: N10,000


★ Loans
Access flexible loan up to N1,000,000 to meet your financial needs. Get fund now to expand your business and support your farm.
Our loan service offers accessible credit for small-scale farmers and entrepreneurs to enhance their businesses. Apply now!


★ Agro Product Sale
Buy our products at affordable price. We have a range of products in our store houses you can purchase (order) from the comfort of your house. We deliver safely and promptly across states in Nigeria


★ Farmers and Customers Connect
We help farmers market their agricultural products, by connecting them to potential customers through our available technology recourses.

Our sustainable agriculture education programs are designed to equip small-scale farmers and entrepreneurs with the knowledge and skills they need to boost crop yields and enhance food security.
