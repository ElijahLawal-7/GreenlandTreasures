About Us

We are a fast-growing agricultural technology company and financial institution, passionate about ensuring food and financial security. We combine savings and investment services with crop production and distribution; to create a more just and equitable world.

Our Mission is to:
• To making the word a healthier, greener and more connect place, through fresh food, smart saving and shared commitment to making a difference.
• To Build the largest community of farmers across Africa and ensure financial stability of her people. .
