WELCOME TO GREENLAND ABUNDANT TREASURES LTD

Notes From The Founder

At Greenland Abundant Treasure, we are excited to be part of the agricultural technology and financial industry in Nigeria and Africa. As an agricultural technology company and financial institution, we are dedicated to ensuring food and financial security for our customers. We believe in the power of combining savings and investment services with crop production and distribution to create a more just and equitable world.

The agricultural industry in Nigeria and Africa is a vital sector that plays a significant role in the economy. Agriculture accounts for a significant portion of the GDP and employs a large percentage of the population. With a growing population and increasing demand for food, there is a need for innovative solutions to enhance agricultural productivity and ensure sustainable practices.

Our focus on sustainable and ethical practices sets us apart in the industry. We are committed to producing high-quality and healthy crops and livestock, ensuring that our customers receive the best products. Our online and physical distribution channels make it easy for customers across Nigeria and Africa to access fresh and healthy produce.

Furthermore, our platform serves as a bridge between small-scale farmers and entrepreneurs and customers. We believe in fair distribution of agricultural produce, connecting farmers directly with customers, and eliminating middlemen. This not only benefits the farmers but also ensures that customers receive fair prices and supports local communities.
